Product Featuress

Text Messaging

Users can send and receive text messages, including emojis, stickers, and formatted text (such as bold, italics, and strikethrough).

Voice Calling

WhatsApp allows users to make voice calls over the internet, enabling free communication between users regardless of their location.

Video Calling

In addition to voice calls, WhatsApp supports video calls, allowing users to have face-to-face conversations with friends, family, or colleagues.

Group Chats

Users can create and participate in group chats with multiple contacts, making it easy to have discussions, collaborations, or social interactions with a group of people.

Multimedia Sharing

WhatsApp enables users to share a variety of multimedia content, including photos, videos, audio files, and documents (such as PDFs and Word documents).

Voice Message

Users can record and send voice messages within chats, making it convenient for quick communication or when typing is not feasible.

Location Sharing

Share Live Location

When you select "Share Live Location," you can choose the duration for which your live location will be shared (15 minutes, 1 hour, or 8 hours). Avilon will continuously update your location on the map, showing your real-time movement to the recipients. Recipients can view your live location on the map and track your movement until the sharing duration expire

Share Current Location

Choosing "Send Your Current Location" will share your static location at that moment. This option is useful for sharing a specific point on the map, such as a meetup location, restaurant, or landmark.

More Features

Status Update

Avilon has a feature called "Status" where users can share updates, photos, videos, or text-based statuses that disappear after 24 hours. This feature is similar to stories on other social media platforms.

Avilon Web

Avilon offers a web-based platform called Avilon Web, which allows users to access their Avilon account on a computer or laptop using a web browser. This feature syncs conversations between the mobile app and the web version.

Avilon Business

WhatsApp Business is a separate app designed for small businesses, offering features like business profiles, automated messages (e.g., greetings, away messages, quick replies), messaging statistics, and the ability to interact with customers more efficiently.

Encryption and Privacy

WhatsApp emphasizes user privacy and security by using end-to-end encryption for messages, providing control over profile visibility and contact interactions, and implementing features like two-factor authentication for added security.
